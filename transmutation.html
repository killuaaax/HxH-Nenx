Transmutation

- Transmuters can change the properties of their aura into something else (e.g., texture, shape).
- They excel at creating unique and unpredictable abilities.

Examples :

Killua Zoldyck
Hisoka Morow
Feitan Portor

Key Traits :

  • Creative and flexible
  • Aura can mimic other materials (rubber, electricity, etc.)
  • Great for tricky, deceptive combat styles
