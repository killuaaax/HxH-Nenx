Enhancement

- Enhancers strengthen and reinforce the natural abilities of an object or their own body.
- They excel at improving raw power, speed, and durability, making them well-balanced fighters.

Examples :

Gon Freecss
Isaac Netero
Nobunaga Hazama

Key Traits :

  • Balanced offense and defense
  • Straightforward ability growth
  • Often focus on physical combat
